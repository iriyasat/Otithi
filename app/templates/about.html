About Othiti

Authentic Hospitality

Welcome to অতিথি - Experience Authentic Bangladeshi Hospitality

অতিথি is a community-driven accommodation platform designed for Bangladesh. We connect travelers with real local hosts across the country, promoting hospitality, culture, and economic empowerment.



Our mission is to make travel personal again — by letting guests experience authentic Bangladeshi living, and giving hosts an opportunity to earn and share their culture. We believe that the best way to experience a country is through its people, and we're here to facilitate those meaningful connections.

Othiti is 100% Bangladesh-focused — built for local people and places. Unlike international platforms, we understand the unique cultural context of Bangladesh and design our services accordingly. We focus on authentic experiences, local connections, and community building.

We have a comprehensive verification system for both hosts and guests. Every host is verified through multiple steps including ID verification, property inspection, and community reviews. Our 24/7 support team is always available to help ensure safe and comfortable experiences.

Guests can expect authentic cultural experiences that go beyond typical tourism. Stay with local families, enjoy home-cooked meals, learn about local customs, and create lasting friendships. Our hosts are passionate about sharing their culture and making you feel like part of the family.
